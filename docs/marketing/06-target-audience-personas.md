# 6. Target Audience & Personas

## Persona 1: "Multi-Framework Developer" (freelancer/agency)
- **Profile**: 5+ years of experience, 2-3 frameworks, 3-5 projects
- **Pain**: different debug tools, context switching
- **Message**: *"One tool for all your frameworks"*
- **Channel**: YouTube, Habr, Reddit r/PHP, dev.to

## Persona 2: "Tech Lead" (agency/product team)
- **Profile**: manages a team of 5-15, projects on different stacks
- **Pain**: tool standardization, onboarding
- **Message**: *"Standardize debugging across your team"*
- **Channel**: conferences, LinkedIn, Packagist

## Persona 3: "Microservices Architect"
- **Profile**: distributed systems, PHP + other languages
- **Pain**: no unified debugging picture across services
- **Message**: *"One debug panel for all your microservices"*
- **Channel**: GitHub, tech blogs, conference talks

## Persona 4: "Learning Developer" (junior)
- **Profile**: 0-2 years of experience, learning a framework
- **Pain**: doesn't understand application internals
- **Message**: *"Look inside. Understand how your framework works."*
- **Channel**: YouTube tutorials, Stack Overflow
