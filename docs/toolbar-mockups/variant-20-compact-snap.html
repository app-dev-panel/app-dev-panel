Variant 20 — Compact Snap Widget

Drag anywhere, snap to edges — layout morphs automatically

HYBRID V1+V3+V12+V17 SNAP MORPH

The ultimate hybrid: a compact widget that starts floating (V12), can be dragged (V17), and automatically morphs its layout when snapped to screen edges — bottom becomes a horizontal bar (V1), sides become a vertical rail (V3).

  • Float mode: compact card, drag anywhere, resize from corner
  • Snap bottom: morphs into full-width horizontal bar (V1 style)
  • Snap right/left: morphs into vertical rail (V3 style)
  • Layout transition is animated — chips morph into rows when docking to side
  • Blue snap zone indicators appear when dragging near edges
  • Mode label shows when snapping ("Docked: Bottom", etc.)
  • Remembers last position

Interaction: Drag the header to move → approach an edge to see snap zones → release to dock → drag away to undock → resize corner in float mode.
