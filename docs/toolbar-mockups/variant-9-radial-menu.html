Variant 9 — Radial Pie Menu

Circular context menu for debug metrics

RADICAL SPATIAL ZERO FOOTPRINT

Completely eliminates the toolbar as a persistent UI element. Instead, a radial menu appears on demand — triggered by clicking a floating orb or a keyboard shortcut. Metrics are arranged in a circle around the center hub.

  • Metrics arranged in a circle — spatial memory for quick access
  • Spring animation with staggered delays for each item
  • Glow effect on hover — items enlarge and highlight
  • Center hub shows overall status (duck + status code)
  • Zero persistent footprint — menu appears only when needed
  • Inspired by Blender's pie menus and gaming radial menus
  • Could be triggered by middle-click or custom shortcut

Interaction: Click the orb (bottom-right) or press Ctrl+D → radial menu appears → hover items to see tooltips → click to navigate → click center or Esc to close.
