Variant 2 — Floating Island

Inspired by macOS Dynamic Island / Apple HIG

MODERN PREMIUM DARK-NATIVE

A dark floating capsule anchored to the bottom center, inspired by Apple's Dynamic Island. The toolbar morphs from a compact pill to a wide bar with smooth spring animation.

  • Always dark regardless of page theme — stands out as an overlay tool
  • Pill-shaped chips with subtle glow effects on hover
  • Spring-like expand/collapse animation (cubic-bezier 0.4, 0, 0.1, 1)
  • Panel floats above the island as a separate card, not full-width
  • Horizontal scroll for overflow metrics (no wrapping)
  • Live status dot with pulse animation

Interaction: Click the dark pill (bottom-center) to expand → click metrics → click 📐 to toggle floating panel → click 🦆 to collapse.
